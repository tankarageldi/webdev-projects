Lasagna

Description

This is my mom's special homemade lasagna recipe with a meaty, made-from-scratch tomato sauce and a deliciously cheesy filling. A more traditional homemade lasagna filling would be made with ricotta but my mom's recipe calls for a blend of small-curd cottage cheese and Parmesan. I have found none better anywhere. Serve with a leafy green salad and crusty garlic bread.

Ingredients

  • pound of ground meat
  • a diced onion cooked until translucent with the ground meat
  • can of tomato sauce, can of crushed tomatoes
  • two tablespoons of parsley, one clove of garlic
  • a dash of sugar
  • dried basil,dried oregano, salt, and black pepper
  • lasagna noodles
  • cottage cheese and parmesan, and shredded mozarella
  • eggs

Steps

  1. Cook the meat
  2. cook the noodles
  3. make the cheese layer
  4. assemble the lasagna
  5. bake the lasagna
